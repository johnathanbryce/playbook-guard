MASTER SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT — Aptonyx Software, Inc. ("Provider") and Customer

1. DEFINITIONS
1.1 "Service" means Provider's hosted Aptonyx platform, including its AI features. "Customer Data" means data, inputs, and materials submitted to the Service by or on behalf of Customer. "DPA" means the Data Processing Addendum referenced in Section 6.

2.1 Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use the Service during the Term for its internal business purposes, subject to this Agreement and the applicable Order Form.

3.1 Customer is responsible for the legality of Customer Data, for use of the Service by its authorized users, and for safeguarding its account credentials.

4. FEES AND PAYMENT
4.1 Customer shall pay the annual subscription fee of $220,000 as set forth in the Order Form, invoiced annually in advance.
4.2 Invoices are due within thirty (30) days of the invoice date (Net 30). Undisputed late amounts accrue interest at 1.0% per month.
4.3 Fees for the then-current term are fixed. Provider may not change pricing during a subscription term. All fees are exclusive of taxes.

5. TERM, AUTO-RENEWAL AND PRICING
5.1 The subscription term is twelve (12) months from the subscription start date stated in the Order Form.
5.2 The subscription automatically renews for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days before the end of the then-current term.
5.3 Provider will send Customer written notice of renewal pricing at least sixty (60) days before each renewal date.
5.4 Any renewal fee increase shall not exceed the lesser of five percent (5%) or the change in the Consumer Price Index (All Urban Consumers) over the preceding twelve (12) months.

6.2 Provider maintains a published list of its sub-processors and will give Customer written notice at least thirty (30) days before adding or replacing a sub-processor. Customer may object on reasonable data-protection grounds within that period; if the objection is not resolved, Customer may terminate the affected portion of the Service and receive a pro-rata refund of prepaid fees.
6.3 Cross-border transfers of personal data are governed by the Standard Contractual Clauses (Module Two, controller-to-processor) incorporated into the DPA.
6.4 Provider will notify Customer of any personal data breach affecting Customer Data without undue delay and in no event later than seventy-two (72) hours after becoming aware of the breach, and will provide information reasonably required for Customer to meet its own notification obligations.

8.1 Provider will make the Service available 99.9% of the time each calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance announced at least 48 hours in advance.
8.2 If monthly availability falls below 99.9%, Customer is entitled to escalating service credits: 5% of the monthly fee for availability between 99.0% and 99.9%; 10% for availability between 98.0% and 99.0%; and 25% for availability below 98.0%.
