MASTER SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT — Northgate Cloudworks, Inc. ("Provider") and Customer

This Master Subscription Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date set forth on the applicable Order Form by and between Northgate Cloudworks, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Provider"), and the customer identified on the Order Form ("Customer"). Provider and Customer are each a "Party" and together the "Parties."

1. DEFINITIONS
1.1 "Services" means Provider's hosted software-as-a-service platform and related support services described in an Order Form.
1.2 "Order Form" means an ordering document executed by the Parties that references this Agreement and specifies the Services, subscription term, and fees.
1.3 "Documentation" means Provider's then-current user guides and technical documentation for the Services.
1.4 "Customer Data" means electronic data submitted to the Services by or on behalf of Customer.
1.5 "Confidential Information" means non-public information disclosed by a Party that is designated confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given its nature and the circumstances of disclosure.

2. SUBSCRIPTION AND LICENSE
2.1 Subject to this Agreement and the applicable Order Form, Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right during the subscription term to access and use the Services for Customer's internal business purposes.
2.2 Customer shall not: (a) sublicense, resell, or make the Services available to third parties except authorized users; (b) reverse engineer or copy any component of the Services; (c) use the Services to build a competing product; or (d) exceed usage limits set forth in the Order Form.

3. CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES
3.1 Customer is responsible for the accuracy and legality of Customer Data and for its authorized users' compliance with this Agreement.
3.2 Customer shall maintain the confidentiality of login credentials and promptly notify Provider of any suspected unauthorized access to Customer's account.
3.3 Customer shall use the Services in compliance with applicable laws and the Documentation.

4. FEES AND PAYMENT
4.1 Customer shall pay the fees specified in each Order Form. Except as expressly stated herein, fees are non-refundable.
4.2 Provider shall invoice annually in advance unless otherwise stated in the Order Form.
4.3 Fees are exclusive of taxes; Customer is responsible for all applicable taxes, excluding taxes on Provider's net income.

5. AUTO-RENEWAL AND PRICING
5.1 The initial subscription term is twelve (12) months from the Effective Date unless otherwise specified in the Order Form.
5.2 The subscription will automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either Party gives written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term.
5.3 Fees for any renewal term shall not increase by more than five percent (5%) over the fees for the immediately preceding term, provided Provider gives notice of any increase at least forty-five (45) days before renewal.
5.4 Invoices are due net thirty (30) days from the invoice date. Undisputed amounts more than thirty (30) days past due may accrue interest at the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law.

6. SERVICE LEVELS AND TERMINATION
6.1 Provider will make the Services available at least 99.9% of the time in each calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance announced at least forty-eight (48) hours in advance and unavailability caused by factors outside Provider's reasonable control ("Uptime Commitment").
6.2 If monthly uptime falls below the Uptime Commitment, Customer's sole and exclusive remedy is a service credit against future fees as follows: below 99.9% but at least 99.5%, a credit of 5% of the monthly fee; below 99.5% but at least 99.0%, a credit of 10%; below 99.0%, a credit of 25%. Credits must be requested within thirty (30) days of the end of the affected month.
6.3 Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice describing the breach.
6.4 Upon termination or expiration, Customer's right to access the Services ceases. For thirty (30) days following termination, Provider will make Customer Data available for export in a commonly used, machine-readable format at no additional charge, after which Provider may delete Customer Data in accordance with its standard practices.
6.5 Sections that by their nature should survive termination shall survive, including Sections 4, 7, 8, and 9.

Customer's exclusive remedy for breach of this warranty is re-performance of the deficient Services or, if Provider cannot cure, termination of the affected Order Form and a pro-rata refund of prepaid, unused fees.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
8.1 Each Party shall protect the other Party's Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care it uses for its own similar information, and no less than reasonable care, and shall use such information only to perform its obligations or exercise its rights under this Agreement.
8.2 Confidential Information excludes information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available without breach; (b) was known to the recipient prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed without use of the discloser's Confidential Information; or (d) is rightfully received from a third party without restriction.
8.3 A Party may disclose Confidential Information to the extent required by law, provided it gives prompt notice (where legally permitted) and reasonable cooperation to seek protective treatment.
